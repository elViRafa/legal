ReadyLocal Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Summary: ReadyLocal is an offline-first emergency preparedness application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external server. All data you create (family plans, checklists, settings) stays on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

ReadyLocal collects no personally identifiable information (PII) and no location data.

  • App preferences & settings — Stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Never transmitted.
  • Family emergency plan data — Stored locally on your device. Never transmitted.
  • Checklist progress — Stored locally on your device. Never transmitted.
  • Subscription status — If you purchase a Pro subscription, RevenueCat (our billing provider) processes the transaction and stores a pseudonymous app user ID to validate entitlements. See Section 4.

2. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — Used solely to control the hardware LED flashlight for the SOS distress beacon feature. No photos or video are captured or stored.
  • Microphone / Audio — Used solely to play emergency siren sounds through the device speaker. No audio is recorded.

No other permissions are requested.

3. Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using React Native AsyncStorage. This data is:

  • Never uploaded to any server operated by ReadyLocal
  • Accessible only to the ReadyLocal application
  • Deleted when you uninstall the application

To erase all local data manually, go to Settings > Clear All Data within the app, or uninstall and reinstall the application.

4. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

If you purchase a ReadyLocal Pro subscription, payments are processed by:

  • Google Play Billing — Google handles all payment processing. ReadyLocal never sees your credit card or payment details. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous app user ID and purchase receipt to validate your subscription. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Purchase history data shared with RevenueCat is used solely to validate your premium subscription status and is encrypted in transit.

5. Third-Party Services

ReadyLocal does not use:

  • Analytics SDKs (no Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that upload personal data
  • Social media integrations

6. Children's Privacy

ReadyLocal is designed for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
